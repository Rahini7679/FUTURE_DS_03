# 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a marketing funnel to understand how users move through different stages of the customer journey—from website sessions to leads, qualified leads, opportunities, and finally customers.

The dashboard helps identify conversion drop-offs, evaluate marketing channel performance, compare campaign effectiveness, and provide actionable business insights for improving customer acquisition.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze the marketing funnel.
- Identify drop-off points.
- Measure conversion performance.
- Compare revenue across marketing channels.
- Evaluate campaign effectiveness.
- Analyze customer sessions by device.
- Provide business recommendations.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- A significant drop-off occurs between website sessions and leads.
- Email marketing generated the highest revenue.
- Paid Search was the second-best performing channel.
- Some campaigns produced considerably more customers than others.
- Customer sessions were distributed across Desktop, Mobile, and Tablet devices.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve landing pages to increase visitor-to-lead conversion.
- Allocate more budget to high-performing channels like Email.
- Optimize low-performing campaigns.
- Improve lead nurturing to convert more leads into customers.
- Continue monitoring funnel performance using interactive dashboards.
